AI-Powered Content Moderation

CoderSpace is committed to maintaining a safe and professional environment. All content posted on our platform is monitored by advanced AI systems to ensure community guidelines are followed.

Prohibited Content

The following types of content are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action:

  • Violent content - Threats, harassment, or promotion of violence
  • Sexual content - Inappropriate or explicit material
  • Offensive behavior - Crude language, discrimination, or toxic attitudes
  • Spam & misleading content - Irrelevant posts or false information

Consequences

Violations may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination. Our AI system learns and improves continuously to better protect our community while allowing constructive technical discussions to flourish.
